Route & Rate Website Redesign — Replit Prompt
Context
You are redesigning the Route & Rate website (routeandrate.com). The existing site was built for a parcel courier tendering service. The business has pivoted — it is now a 3PL invoice audit service for UK DTC ecommerce brands. The structure, colour palette (navy + white + light grey), and general visual style can be preserved, but all copy, framing, and the ICP (ideal customer profile) section need to be replaced from scratch.
The business is in early pilot stage. The founder has a corporate logistics procurement background (Abbott Laboratories, EMEA freight tenders, ~$50M spend), and the thesis is simple: 3PL invoices are complex, systematic overcharges of ~8% are routine, and DTC brands have no one in-house to catch them. The pilot is free — no fee, no catch.
